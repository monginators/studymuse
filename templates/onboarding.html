The Robin Report Guide!

Welcome to the application tutorial. This guide is designed to help users navigate and utilize the application effectively. By following the steps, you'll become proficient in reporting issues and tracking them.

1. Registration and Login

Before you can report an issue, you need to register an account and log in.

2. Reporting an Issue

On the dashboard, click the 'Report Issue' button. Select the type of issue, provide a location, and upload a photo.

3. Viewing Reported Issues

View all the issues you've reported on the dashboard. Here, you'll see details like status, type, and more.

4. Navigating the Map

Use the 'Map' link in the navigation to view a map of all reported issues. Click on individual markers for more details.

5. Earning Points

For each issue you report and other activities, you earn points. Check them on your dashboard.

6. Neighborhood Rankings

Discover how different neighborhoods rank based on the number and type of reported issues. Learn to navigate the rankings page, understand the metrics, and see where your community stands.
